

Start of the game:

The game consists of a set of pictures that are located face down.

Game progress:

The player takes turns choosing two pictures and turns them face up.
If the pictures are the same (have the same meaning), the player gets a point and chooses two other pictures again.
If the pictures are different, they turn face down, you need to try to find the pair again.

Goal of the game:

The goal of the game is to collect as many pairs of identical pictures as possible.
The game ends when all pairs of pictures are found.
